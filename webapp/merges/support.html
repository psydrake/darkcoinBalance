What is Darkcoin?

Darkcoin is the first anonymous digital currency (a digital version of cash). When sending Darkcoin, you choose whether the transaction is private or public. Your Darkcoin are stored in a digital wallet that you own and control. Transactions are anonymous "from the wallet" meaning you don't have to trust a third party to make the transaction private. Check out the Darkcoin website to find out more!
